About Us

Horizon Tech Toys was founded on November 22, 2021. We are developing commercial space-travel themed toys for children. These toys are small enough to fit in a Kinder(TM) Egg when disassembled, and are bigger than a kinder egg when fully assembled. Made out of 3D-printed ABS plastic, these toys provide a cheap, durable method of entertainment to children everywhere.

Our Philosophy

At Horizon, we believe in reaching for the stars with our toys, both literally and figuratively. We believe that in creating the toys, all of our toys should be created at the highest level, and any effort below that is insufficient. These toys are the future of entertainment for children, and it would be shameful to sell any incomplete product.
